Evergreen Engineering and Computer Science Society

Bringing Tech Interest To Evergreen

Evergreen Engineering and Computer Science Society (EECSS) is a non-profit organization and society that aims to promote interest in different engineering and computer science disciplines within the Evergreen community.

EECSS holds free classes at the Evergreen Library that teach students computer programming and the building of electronics. We invite speakers of the different engineering professions to talk about their jobs and life in the technological industry, with opportunities for audience members to ask questions.
